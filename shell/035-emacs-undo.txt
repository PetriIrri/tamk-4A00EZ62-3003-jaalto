How to use undo.

First write a line with a sufficient amount of words. More than two is preferred.
For example: The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy programmer.
now kill two random words with M-d
example: The brown fox jumped over the programmer.
Now undo with C-_
example after undo: The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy programmer.
