How to copy and paste in vi:
in command mode you can use <dd> and <p> to cut and paste a line

To copy and paste a line of text do:
Enter visual line mode using V and select the line(s) that you want to copy.
then press y (yank) to copy the line.
Pressing p (paste) will paste the line.
